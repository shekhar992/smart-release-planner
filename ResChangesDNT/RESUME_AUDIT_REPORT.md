## 🎯 Key Issues with Current Resume (Why You're Not Getting Calls)

### 1. **Generic Professional Summary**
- **Current:** Long paragraph that reads like every other PM resume
- **Impact:** ATS systems and recruiters skip it; wastes top real estate
- **Fix:** Remove it entirely OR replace with a 1-line value proposition

### 2. **Weak Quantification**
- **Current:** Soft metrics like "~50%", "10+ features", "40% adoption"
- **Reference:** Hard numbers: "$1M", "107% YoY growth", "3,000 patients in 2 months"
- **Impact:** Your achievements don't stand out; they blend in
- **Fix:** Add concrete dollar amounts, user counts, time saved

### 3. **Buried Impact**
- **Current:** Achievements mixed with responsibilities
- **Reference:** Every bullet is a measurable achievement
- **Impact:** Hiring managers can't quickly see your value
- **Fix:** Lead every bullet with impact, then explain how

### 4. **Weak Action Verbs**
- **Current:** "Owned", "Led", "Managed" (overused PM words)
- **Reference:** "Established", "Built and automated", "Closed", "Launched"
- **Impact:** Resume sounds passive and generic
- **Fix:** Use stronger, specific verbs that show building/creating

### 5. **Layout Issues**
- **Current:** Center-aligned header (wastes space)
- **Reference:** Left-right justified header (professional, space-efficient)
- **Impact:** Looks less executive, harder to scan
- **Fix:** Restructure header layout

### 6. **Missing Context**
- **Current:** Just lists features built
- **Reference:** Shows business context (e.g., "for Big Four advertising company")
- **Impact:** Unclear who benefited and why it mattered
- **Fix:** Add stakeholder context to each role
